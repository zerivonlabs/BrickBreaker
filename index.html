Privacy Policy

For Block Breaker

Last updated: March 14, 2026
1

Information We Collect

Block Breaker ("the App") by Zerivon Labs prioritizes your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, email, phone number, or social media accounts. The core gameplay operates offline, while online features utilize anonymous identifiers.

🔒 We collect zero personally identifiable data. Your privacy is fully protected.

2

Online Features & Data Usage

To enhance your experience, the App utilizes the following online functionalities:

  • 🎮 Leaderboards: We use Anonymous Login to provide competitive rankings. When you participate, a random identifier is created. Only your highest score and a generic placeholder name are stored on our secure servers.
  • ⚙️ Remote Configuration: The game occasionally connects to our servers to fetch minor parameter adjustments (e.g., gameplay balance, ball speed). This process transmits no personal data from your device.
  • 🛠️ Crash Diagnostics: To improve app stability, we automatically collect anonymous crash reports. These logs only contain the device state (OS version, model, stack trace) at the time of the crash.
3

Local Data Storage

Your immediate game progress — including current score, level, and ball count — is saved locally on your device. This cached data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled.

4

Third-Party Services

This app uses trusted third-party services to power its online features. These services have their own Privacy Policies:

  • • Google Play Services
  • • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics
  • • Firebase Remote Config & Firestore

Learn more about Google's and Firebase's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy and firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5

Advertising

This app does not display any advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.

6

Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As no personal data is collected, the app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update and the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
